Available Game Statuses

[scheduled] | scheduled to occur

[inprogress] | first pitch of game has been thrown

[complete] | last pitch of game, game stats under review

[closed] | game has passed stats review after last pitch

[wdelay] | delayed due to weather

[fdelay] | delayed due to facility issues

[odelay] | game has been delayed

[canceled] | game canceled, no makeup game

[unnecessary] | scheduled series game but not needed due to a team clinching series

[if-necessary] | game will be scheduled if required

[postponed] | game postponed and will be made up

[suspended] | game suspended and will be rescheduled where left off

[maintenance] | game failed review and is being repaired
